# Business Overview

## What Superspeed Is

Programmatic GHL campaign deployment engine. Define campaigns in Python, deploy 8 workflows / 45 steps in ~3 seconds.

## Who It Serves

Doctor LeadFlow (DLF) clients — health practices, med spas, IV wellness, fitness, etc. Every client gets standardized nurture/follow-up campaigns that previously took hours to build by hand.

## Why It Exists

Manual workflow building in GHL is the bottleneck for client onboarding. Superspeed turns a 4-hour build into a 4-second deploy, which means new clients can be fully campaign-ready inside a single onboarding call.

## Position in the DLF Stack

```
Lead source (ads / referrals)
   ↓
GHL CRM (contacts, tags)
   ↓
Superspeed campaigns ← THIS REPO
   ↓
Booking / fulfillment (services, offers)
```
