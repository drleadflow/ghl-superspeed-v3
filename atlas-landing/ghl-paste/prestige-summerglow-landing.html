Frequently asked questions

What exactly is the Summer Glow Protocol?

A personalized 8-week program combining a series of medical-grade skin treatments (chosen for your skin's specific needs — texture, tone, hydration, overall glow) with a customized wellness plan built from your health history and goals. Everything is selected at your consultation — nothing is one-size-fits-all.

Is this just fancy facials?

No. The skin treatments are clinical-grade — they work at a level over-the-counter products and day-spa facials can't reach. Combined with internal wellness support, results compound in ways surface-only approaches don't.

Who is a good candidate?

Women who've noticed changes in their skin or body — especially through their 40s and 50s — and want a structured, medical plan instead of guesswork. Candidacy is confirmed at your consultation; if Andrea doesn't think it's the right fit, she'll tell you and point you in the right direction.

How fast will I see results?

The protocol runs 8 weeks, and many patients report visible changes within it. Every plan and every timeline is personal — individual results vary, and Andrea will set honest expectations for yours at the consultation.

What does it cost?

The consultation is free, and your plan is personalized — so the investment depends on what your plan actually includes. Andrea builds what makes sense for you, nothing more. You'll know exactly what's involved before committing to anything.

Do I have to be in Franklin?

The clinic serves Franklin, Brentwood, Cool Springs, and the greater Nashville area. Treatments are in-person at A Prestige Aesthetics & Wellness in Franklin, TN.
