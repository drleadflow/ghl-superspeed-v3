No. There are no contracts — cancel anytime, no questions asked. The membership exists so you keep member pricing on Botox, Dysport, fillers, and skincare for as long as you want it.

What if I prefer Dysport?

Members get Dysport at $4/unit (typically $6–8 elsewhere). Your provider will recommend the right product for your face and goals at your visit.

Most first visits cover the forehead, frown lines, and crow's feet — typically 35–65 units total. Use the calculator above for your areas; your exact plan is set by our medical team before a single unit is placed.

Will I look natural?

That's the entire philosophy here. Dosing is mapped to how your face actually moves. Patients tell us people notice they look rested — but can't pinpoint why.

Is it real Botox?

Yes — Botox is Botox. It comes from the same manufacturer (Allergan) whether you're at a "luxury" Galleria med spa or here. The only difference is the markup. You're not paying for better Botox at those fancy places — you're paying for their waiting room.

Does it hurt? What's the downtime?

A quick pinch, over in seconds — the needles are extremely fine. Virtually no downtime; most patients go straight back to their day.

How long do results last?

Initial smoothing starts within days, full results settle around 2 weeks, and most patients hold results 3–4 months.
