## Core Promise

In about 10 days, a fully individualized longevity blueprint built from your intake plus 100+ biomarkers: your 10-year and 30-year heart attack / stroke risk (AHA PREVENT equation refined by risk-enhancing factors), your 8-year diabetes risk (LPIR, which can detect insulin resistance about 8 years before A1c moves), your hormone and metabolic status, and a personalized lifestyle-medicine plan (nutrition, movement, sleep, stress) with the highest-leverage interventions to start immediately.

## What's Included

- 100+ biomarker panel: cardiometabolic, hormone, thyroid, inflammatory, and advanced lipids including ApoB and Lp(a)
- Mobile phlebotomy at your home or office (included), so you never sit in a lab waiting room
- 60-minute physician consult with Dr. Joel Wussow MD
- Detailed intake review: lifestyle, family and personal history, and risk-enhancing factors
- Multi-guideline cardiovascular risk stratification (10-year and 30-year)
- 8-year diabetes risk assessment (LPIR, lipoprotein insulin resistance)
- Cardiometabolic phenotyping: insulin sensitivity, visceral adiposity, atherogenic dyslipidemia, inflammatory burden
- Full hormone evaluation interpreted against optimal ranges, not population-average ranges
- Nutrition, training, sleep, stress, and recovery analysis tied to your biomarkers
- Targeted supplement and pharmacotherapy recommendations where clinically indicated
- A written plain-language Patient PWA Report with a prioritized action plan
- Concierge follow-up pathway: retest cadence and longitudinal optimization
