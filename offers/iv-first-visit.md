# IV Wellness First-Visit Offer

## What's Included

- 15% off any drip on the first visit
- Free add-on of choice (Red Light, Compression Boots, or PEMF mat)
- Free pre-drip wellness check, included

## Deliverables

A discounted first mobile drip session in the lead's home/hotel/office (within ~15 miles of Cincinnati), performed by a licensed RN, with one of the three add-ons layered on for free and a brief on-site wellness check before anything goes in.

## Fulfillment

- Booking via `drip.iv-wellness.com/book` or via the Ashley concierge bot
- RN dispatched to the address on the booked day; texts 30 minutes before arrival to confirm
- Standard visit length: 45 to 60 minutes (IV stick stings ~10 seconds, drip runs the rest)
- Setup, drip, cleanup all on-site

## Terms

- Payment: card on file or pay at the visit ("either works, no charge until the day")
- Out-of-pocket only — IV hydration is not covered by insurance, same as most aesthetic and wellness services
- No Care Credit, no financing
- Offer is first-visit only
