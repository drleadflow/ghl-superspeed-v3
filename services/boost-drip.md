# Boost Drip

## Description

Entry-level hydration and vitamin drip. Most flexible option for first-time clients — pairs cleanly with the free pre-drip wellness check, which lets the on-site RN flag a different drip if Boost isn't the right fit that day. Total visit runs 45 to 60 minutes.

Pricing: $175 (lowest entry point in the IV Wellness drip menu).

## Who It's For

- First-time IV clients with no specific complaint ("just generally tired", "run-down", "busy stretch")
- Anyone unsure which drip is right for them — the wellness check pivots to the right drip if Boost isn't the call

## Booking Notes

- Buffer before/after: not stated
- Provider availability constraints: 100% mobile within ~15 miles of Cincinnati. Outside that radius the lead is unbookable as mobile (apply `iv-out-of-area` tag).
- Required intake form / pre-call docs: free pre-drip wellness check is performed on-site before anything goes in, included with every first visit
