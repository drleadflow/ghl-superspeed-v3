# Pre-drip Wellness Check

## Description

On-site clinical screening performed by the licensed RN before any drip goes in. Roughly 5 minutes (per the AI bot's "5-minute wellness check" framing in Step 5 of the conversation flow). Free and included on every first visit. The check exists so the on-site nurse can flag if a different drip fits better than what the lead booked, or if a drip isn't the right call that day at all.

## Who It's For

Every first-visit lead. Used heavily as an objection-handler in the 28-day nurture: "you don't pay for something that isn't right for you that day." Also the AI bot's go-to response when a lead surfaces value doubt ("am I sure I want this?").

## Booking Notes

- Buffer before/after: built into the drip session — not booked separately
- Provider availability constraints: performed by the on-site RN at the start of every mobile visit
- Required intake form / pre-call docs: none beyond the standard tablet paperwork at the start of the visit
